## Phase 2: Retention & Stickiness (Month 2-3)

**Goal: Get users to come back. Build habit formation.**

### Features to Add (Priority Order):

#### 1. **Progress Tracking** üìä
**What:** Visual dashboard showing mastery per topic/class
- "You're 67% ready for Biology Exam 2"
- Color-coded topics (red = weak, yellow = okay, green = mastered)
- Historical performance graph

**Why:** 
- Gives students tangible sense of progress
- Creates anxiety relief ("I'm actually ready!")
- Encourages continued use

**Impact:** +10% retention

---

#### 2. **Spaced Repetition System** üîÑ
**What:** AI schedules review sessions based on forgetting curve
- "You learned mitosis 5 days ago - time to review"
- Smart scheduling: review right before you'd forget
- Integrated into dashboard ("3 reviews due today")

**Why:**
- Transforms from "cram tool" to "learning system"
- Creates daily habit loop
- Backed by cognitive science (actually works)

**Impact:** +20% retention, shifts positioning to "serious learning tool"

---

#### 3. **More Question Types** ‚ùì
**What:** Beyond multiple choice
- Fill-in-the-blank
- True/false
- Short essay prompts (AI grades for key concepts)
- Matching/ordering questions

**Why:**
- Different question types test different depths of knowledge
- Reduces monotony
- Better mimics real exam formats

**Impact:** +5% satisfaction

---

#### 4. **Study Session Mode** ‚è±Ô∏è
**What:** Guided cram sessions with timer
- "30-minute bio cram session"
- Pomodoro-style breaks
- Adaptive difficulty (starts easy, gets harder)
- End summary: "You improved 23% in this session"

**Why:**
- Structure reduces overwhelm
- Gamifies the grind
- Clear start/end points

**Impact:** +15% engagement

---

#### 5. **Streak Counter & Gamification** üî•
**What:** Duolingo-style streaks
- Daily study streak
- XP for completed quizzes
- Badges/achievements ("Crammed for 5 exams!")
- Leaderboard (optional, friends only)

**Why:**
- FOMO keeps people coming back
- Social proof ("my friends are studying")
- Dopamine hits = retention

**Impact:** +25% retention

**Note:** Keep it subtle. Don't force gamification on serious studiers.
